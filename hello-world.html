About Me:

My name is Leah Jacobs; a graduate of the Welsh Institute of Chiropractic with a Masters in Chiropractic and is currently completing a Post Graduate Certificate in Chiropractic Professional Development. My love of Chiropractic stemmed from personal experience after receiving treatment at a young age. I have always participated in sport and for a while I've had my heart set on becoming a professional sportswoman which resulted in recurrent injuries. I tried a variety of treatments, none with any lasting results, until I sought chiropractic care. Not only did the injuries resolve but it also helped me realise how the body worked and therefore how to treat it, without the need for drugs or surgery. Due to the positive effects that Chiropractic care brought into my life, I felt a strong desire to help others and since this time I have enjoyed treating patients of all ages and all walks of life. I am fully registered with the General Chiropractic Council and a member of the British Chiropractic Association and the Royal College of Chiropractors.
